Myanmar Culture Restaurant

လက်ဖက်သုတ်
(La-Phat Salad)

the fermented tea leaves were at the center, surrounded by crackling-crisp fried garlic chips, crunchy dried lentils and toasted peanuts, savory dried shrimp, nutty sesame seeds, chopped green chilies, fresh tomatoes, and ginger. With its perfect balance of bright, tart lime juice and deep, earthy, salty fish sauce, it was, without question, the favorite salad in my life.
